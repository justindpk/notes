# Kumar

### Notes

- Crumble butter in flour first
- Mix all other ingredients afterwards

## Ingredients

### Dough

- 2 cups of flour 
- 1 cup/small can of pet milk
- 1 tsp vanilla essence
- 3 tbsp of butter
- 2 tablespoons of condensed milk

### Sugar Coat

- 1/2 cup of water
- 1 1/2 cups of sugar
- stalk of ginger ground
- cinnamon
